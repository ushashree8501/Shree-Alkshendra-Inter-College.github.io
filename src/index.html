About School

Established in year 1946, Shree Alakshendra Inter College is located in Rural area of Uttar Pradesh state/ut of India. In Bhinga area of Shravasti block of Shravasti District. Area pincode is 271831.

School is providing Higher Secondary level education and is being managed by Government Aided Organisation.
Medium of instruction is Hindi language and School is Co-educational.
School is affiliated with State Board for both Secondary and High Secondary level.
